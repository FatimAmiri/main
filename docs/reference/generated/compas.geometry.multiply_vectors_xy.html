multiply_vectors_xy

compas.geometry.multiply_vectors_xy(u, v)[source]

Element-wise multiplication of two vectors assumed to lie in the XY plane.

Parameters:
  • u (list) – The XY(Z) components of the first vector.
  • v (list) – The XY(Z) components of the second vector.
Returns:

list – Resulting vector in the XY plane.

Examples

>>>
multiply_vectors norm_vector
