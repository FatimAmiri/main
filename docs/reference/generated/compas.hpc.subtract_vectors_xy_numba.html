hpc.subtract_vectors_xy_numba

hpc.subtract_vectors_xy_numba

Subtract one vector from another, assuming they lie in the XY plane.

Parameters:
  • u (array) – XY(Z) components of the first vector.
  • v (array) – XY(Z) components of the second vector.
Returns:

array – u - v (Z = 0.0).
