geometry

norm_vector_numba Calculate the L2 norm or length of a vector.
norm_vectors_numba Calculate the L2 norm or length of vectors.
length_vector_numba Calculate the length of a vector.
length_vector_xy_numba Calculate the length of the vector, assuming it lies in the XY plane.
length_vector_sqrd_numba Calculate the squared length of the vector.
length_vector_sqrd_xy_numba Calculate the squared length of the vector, assuming it lies in the XY plane.
scale_vector_numba Scale a vector by a given factor.
scale_vector_xy_numba Scale a vector by a given factor, assuming it lies in the XY plane.
scale_vectors_numba Scale multiple vectors by a given factor.
scale_vectors_xy_numba Scale multiple vectors by a given factor, assuming they lie in the XY plane
normalize_vector_numba Normalize a given vector.
normalize_vector_xy_numba Normalize a given vector, assuming it lies in the XY-plane.
normalize_vectors_numba Normalise multiple vectors.
normalize_vectors_xy_numba Normalise multiple vectors, assuming they lie in the XY plane.
power_vector_numba Raise a vector to the given power.
power_vectors_numba Raise multiple vectors to the given power.
square_vector_numba Raise a single vector to the power 2.
square_vectors_numba Raise multiple vectors to the power 2.
add_vectors_numba Add two vectors.
add_vectors_xy_numba Add two vectors, assuming they lie in the XY-plane.
subtract_vectors_numba Subtract one vector from another.
subtract_vectors_xy_numba Subtract one vector from another, assuming they lie in the XY plane.
multiply_vectors_numba Element-wise multiplication of two vectors.
multiply_vectors_xy_numba Element-wise multiplication of two vectors assumed to lie in the XY plane..
divide_vectors_numba Element-wise division of two vectors.
divide_vectors_xy_numba Element-wise division of two vectors assumed to lie in the XY plane.
cross_vectors_numba Compute the cross product of two vectors.
cross_vectors_xy_numba Compute the cross product of two vectors, assuming they lie in the XY-plane.
dot_vectors_numba Compute the dot product of two vectors.
dot_vectors_xy_numba Compute the dot product of two vectors, assuming they lie in the XY-plane.
multiply_matrices_numba The multiplication of matrices.
multiply_matrix_vector_numba The multiplication of a matrix with a vector.
