VolMesh.cell_pair_halffaces

VolMesh.cell_pair_halffaces(ckey_1, ckey_2)

Given 2 ckeys, returns the interfacing halffaces, respectively.

Parameters

  • ckey_1 (hashable) – Identifier of the cell 1.

  • ckey_2 (hashable) – Identifier of the cell 2.

Returns

  • hfkey_1 – The identifier of the halfface belonging to cell 1 .

  • hfkey_2 – The identifier of the halfface belonging to cell 2.
